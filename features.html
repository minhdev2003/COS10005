Features

  1. Highlighted the menu item in the navigator corresponding to the current web page. Click here to see the implement
  2. In order.html, a checkbox with the label 'same as delivery address' is created. When this checkbox is checked, the fields for billing address will be filled with the delivery address automatically. If the delivery address is not completed when this checkbox is checked, an alert will be displayed: 'Please enter your delivery address first'. Click here to see the implement
  3. Allow users to choose from three types of credit card: Visa, Mastercard and American Express. Based on the type of credit card, limit the length of the credit card number: 16 digits for Visa and Mastercard, 15 digits for American Express. Click here to see the implement
